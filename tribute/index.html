Nuevos Dolores

Nuevos Dolores – "New Pains" in English – is an up and rising indie pop band based in Madrid, Spain gaining traction in 46 countries and counting.

Nuevos Dolores combines 60's pop and folk with an eclectic mix of genres and influences; but always with its own distinct sound and far from being a mere exercise in retro style.

In 2015, Jorge Castejón (guitarist and founder) moved from Zaragoza to Madrid, and under the name of Nuevos Dolores, he began recording home-produced songs with melodies reminiscent of the 60's and arrangements of spirited organ and synth from his apartment.

Upon leaving Grupo de Inventores, and releasing various EPs, Nuevos Dolores transformed into a project led principally by Jorge. In June 2019, he released a double single "La Luz/Quizá", that played on Radio 3 and is included on official playlists on Spotify. It is the first of various singles that are included in a self-titled album in 2020: his first appearance on the label Zaragoza Feliz Feliz Records.

During the second half of 2020, Castejón has brought various independent singles to light that are probably his best songs to date.
